sidebarTitle: "Overview"

---

The moment your challenge equity hits the profit target and you've met the minimum trading days requirement with no rule violations, your challenge is marked as passed and a funded account is created automatically. There is no second phase, no identity verification step, and no waiting period — you can start trading your funded account straight away.

## Your new funded account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Challenge passes">
    Your equity reaches the 30% profit target and you've traded on at least 7 distinct days. Any open positions on the challenge are automatically closed at that point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Funded account opens">
    A new funded account is created at the same starting balance as your challenge — for example, if you ran a $50,000 challenge you get a $50,000 funded account. The funded account is a completely separate account; challenge trades and funded trades never mix.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    Your funded account is active immediately. You trade any live Polymarket market available on PolyFundr, under the same risk rules that governed your challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request a payout">
    Once your realized profit on the funded account reaches the 30% target, go to the **Payouts** page and submit a payout request. You keep 90% of realized profit; PolyFundr keeps 10%.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key differences from the challenge

| | Challenge | Funded account |
|---|---|---|
| Purpose | Prove your edge | Trade with PolyFundr capital |
| Profit split | n/a | 90% to you |
| Payout target | Pass the challenge | 30% realized profit per cycle |
| Min trading days | 7 (to pass) | 7 (between payouts) |
| Drawdown rules | Same | Same |
| Account separation | — | Completely separate from challenge |

<Info>
  Your funded account balance starts fresh — it is not seeded with the profits from your challenge. Challenge profits are not transferable to the funded account.
</Info>

## Account sizes

Funded accounts are available at the same sizes as challenges: $10,000, $25,000, $50,000, $100,000, and $200,000. The size of your funded account always matches the challenge you passed.

<Tip>
  If you want a larger funded account, purchase and pass a challenge at the desired size.
</Tip>
