---

Your funded account is where you trade with PolyFundr's capital and keep 90% of what you earn. Once you pass the challenge, a funded account opens automatically at the same size — no waiting period, no verification step, no extra hoops. The same clear risk rules carry over, and when your realized profit reaches the payout threshold you can request USDC on Polygon to the wallet address you enter for that request.
