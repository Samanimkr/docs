Your funded account is where you trade with PolyFundr's capital and keep 90% of what you earn. Once you pass the challenge, a funded account opens automatically at the same size — no waiting period, no verification step, no extra hoops. The same clear risk rules carry over, and when your realized profit hits the target you can request a payout directly to your wallet or bank.
