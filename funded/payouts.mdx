Payouts are how you withdraw your share of profits from your funded account. You can request a payout as soon as your **realized profit** reaches 30% of your starting balance — open positions don't count until they're closed. PolyFundr processes payout requests within 24 hours and sends funds directly to the destination you specify.

## Profit split

Every payout is divided between you and PolyFundr:

- **90%** goes to you
- **10%** goes to PolyFundr

For example, if your realized profit is $5,000 on a $50,000 account — exactly at the 30% target — your payout is $4,500.

## When you can request a payout

You can submit a payout request when both of the following are true:

1. Your **realized profit** has reached the profit target (30% of your starting balance).
2. You have traded on at least **7 distinct days** since your funded account was activated or since your last payout.

<Note>
  Only realized profit counts toward the payout threshold. The value of open positions is excluded until those positions are closed.
</Note>

## How to request a payout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Close any positions you want included">
    Payout calculations use realized profit only. Close the positions you want to count before submitting a request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Payouts page">
    In the app, go to **Payouts** from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your destination wallet address">
    Provide the wallet address you want the USDC sent to. This address is not saved — you must enter it with every request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Review the payout amount and confirm. Once submitted, the requested amount is reserved against your available balance while the payout is pending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your funds">
    PolyFundr processes the request within 24 hours. On approval, USDC is transferred on-chain to the destination address you provided. If you requested a bank transfer, funds arrive via the bank transfer method instead.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="USDC (crypto wallet)">
    Funds are sent as USDC to the wallet address you provide at the time of the request. Enter the full address carefully — the destination is not saved between requests and transfers cannot be reversed once sent.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bank transfer">
    You can also request payment by bank transfer. Select this option when submitting your payout request and follow the prompts to provide your bank details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How pending payouts affect your balance

When you submit a request, the requested amount is **reserved** against your available balance immediately. This means:

- You cannot use those funds to open new positions while the payout is pending.
- Your drawdown floors are calculated after accounting for payouts already made, so withdrawing profits never moves you closer to a drawdown breach.

<Warning>
  Always provide the correct destination wallet address. Payout destinations are not saved, and on-chain USDC transfers cannot be recalled once sent.
</Warning>

## Payout cycles

Each time a payout is paid, the profit target resets. You start a new 30% cycle from your current balance, and the 7-day minimum trading requirement applies again before your next payout.

## Processing time

Payouts are processed within 24 hours of submission. You'll receive an in-app notification when your payout is approved and the transfer is sent.
