Trading runs 24/7 with no restrictions on news events, weekends, or holding periods. You can hold a position for as long as you like, as long as you stay within the risk rules.

## Quick facts

- One-step challenge
- Purchasable account sizes: \$10,000, \$25,000, \$50,000 (\$100k and \$200k via competitions and giveaways)
- Same-size funded account after you pass
- 90% profit split on funded payouts
- USDC payouts on Polygon
- No KYC required to start

## Challenge tiers

| Account size | One-time fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| \$10,000 | \$125 |
| \$25,000 | \$300 |
| \$50,000 | \$500 |

\$100,000 and \$200,000 accounts are not on direct sale right now. They are awarded through competitions and giveaways.

## What makes it simple

- You pass one challenge. There is no second phase.
- Markets are live 24/7.
- The rules are public and fixed.
- Funded payouts use a clear 90% split.
- The same help center covers the full flow, from demo to payout.
