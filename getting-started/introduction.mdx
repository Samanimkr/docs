PolyFundr is the world's first proprietary trading platform for Polymarket prediction markets. Instead of being limited by how much capital you have, you pay a one-time fee to access a simulated challenge account, prove you can trade profitably within a clear set of rules, and receive a real funded account — no KYC required.

## How you get funded

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy a challenge">
    Pay a one-time fee to open a simulated trading account ranging from $10,000 to $200,000. No subscription, no hidden charges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass the challenge">
    Hit a 30% profit target across at least 7 trading days without breaching any risk limits. The moment both conditions are met, you pass automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a funded account">
    A new funded account at the same size opens immediately — no second phase, no verification step, no waiting period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request payouts">
    Once your realized profit reaches the target, request a payout. You keep 90% of profits, paid in USDC to your wallet or by bank transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What makes PolyFundr different

Most prop firms impose multi-phase evaluations, market-hours restrictions, hidden rules, and slow payouts. PolyFundr is built differently:

- **One-step challenge** — pass once, get funded. No second phase.
- **24/7 markets** — prediction markets never close. There are no session limits.
- **No KYC** — start trading without identity verification.
- **Transparent rules** — every limit is documented. No hidden clauses.
- **Clear profit split** — 90% to you, always. No tiered splits.
