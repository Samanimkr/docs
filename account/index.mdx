Your PolyFundr account brings together your identity, your wallet, and your activity in one place. When you sign in for the first time, your account is created automatically — no forms to fill out, no KYC required to start a challenge. From there, a wallet is set up for you, and you can start receiving notifications about your challenges and payouts.

The pages in this section cover the full account experience: how to sign in, how your wallet works, and how PolyFundr keeps you informed.
