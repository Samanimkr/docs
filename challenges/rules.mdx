Your challenge account is governed by two sets of rules: **pass conditions** you must meet to complete the challenge, and **fail conditions** that end it immediately if breached. You need to satisfy every pass condition simultaneously, and you must never trigger any fail condition.

## Pass conditions

You pass the challenge the moment both of the following are true at the same time, with no rule violations on record.

| Condition | Requirement |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Profit target** | Your account equity reaches **+30%** above your starting balance |
| **Minimum trading days** | You have placed at least one qualifying trade on **7 distinct calendar days** |

A qualifying trade is any trade with a notional value of **$1 or more**. Trading days are counted in UTC — two trades placed on the same UTC calendar day count as one trading day.

<Note>
Both conditions must be satisfied at the same time. Reaching the profit target on day 5 does not pass the challenge — you still need to reach 7 trading days with the equity at or above the target.
</Note>

## Fail conditions

Any one of the following ends your challenge immediately and permanently.

| Condition | Limit | Measured from |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Max daily drawdown** | **10%** drop from the day's starting equity | UTC-day equity snapshot |
| **Max total drawdown** | **20%** drop from your all-time peak equity | Trailing high-water mark |
| **Max risk per trade** | **5%** of your starting balance per trade | Before every new trade |
| **Max inactivity** | **14 days** without a qualifying trade | Challenge activation or last trade |

### Max daily drawdown

At the start of each UTC day, the platform snapshots your account equity. If your equity falls more than 10% below that snapshot at any point during the day, the account fails.

**Example:** You start a UTC day with \$52,000 in equity. Your daily floor is \$52,000 x 0.90 = \$46,800. If your equity drops to $46,799 at any point that day, the challenge ends.

<Warning>
The daily drawdown resets each UTC day using a fresh equity snapshot. A large gain on one day raises the floor for the next day.
</Warning>

### Max total drawdown (trailing)

The platform tracks your highest-ever equity. If your equity falls more than **20% below that all-time peak**, the account fails. The high-water mark only moves up — once you reach a new equity peak, the floor rises with it and never comes back down.

**Example:** Your starting balance is \$50,000 and your equity peaks at \$58,000. Your total drawdown floor is `$58,000 x 0.80 = $46,400`. If your equity falls to \$46,399, the challenge ends.

<Info>
Drawdown calculations are adjusted for any payouts already made. Withdrawing profits on a funded account does not move you closer to a breach.
</Info>

### Max risk per trade

Before every trade, the platform calculates your worst-case loss across all open positions on the same market plus the new trade you are about to place. If that combined worst-case loss exceeds 5% of your starting balance, the trade is blocked.

**Example:** On a $50,000 challenge, 5% is $2,500. If you already have $1,800 at risk on a market and try to add a trade that risks another $900 on the same market, the total would be $2,700 — the trade is blocked.

<Tip>
The 5% limit is calculated from your **starting balance**, not your current equity. It does not change as your account grows or shrinks.
</Tip>

### Max inactivity

If 14 or more days pass without a qualifying trade ($1+ notional), the account fails. The clock starts at challenge activation and resets with every qualifying trade you place.

## Rules that carry over to funded accounts

The same rules apply to your funded account after you pass. The profit target becomes a per-payout-cycle target, and the minimum 7 trading days resets between payouts.

| Rule | Challenge | Funded account |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Profit target | 30% of starting balance | 30% per payout cycle |
| Daily drawdown | 10% of day's starting equity | 10% of day's starting equity |
| Total drawdown | 20% trailing from peak | 20% trailing from peak |
| Max risk per trade | 5% of starting balance | 5% of starting balance |
| Min trading days | 7 distinct days | 7 distinct days (between payouts) |
| Max inactivity | 14 days | 14 days |

## What is allowed

- Trade any market available on PolyFundr using live Polymarket prices.
- Hold positions for as long as you like, provided you stay within the rules.
- Trade on news events, weekends, or any time of day.
- No restrictions on holding periods or market categories.
