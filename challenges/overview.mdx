PolyFundr offers five challenge tiers ranging from $10,000 to $200,000. Each tier has a single one-time fee — there are no recurring charges, activation fees, or hidden costs. Once you pass, you receive a funded account at the same starting balance and keep 90% of all realized profits.

## Challenge tiers

The $50,000 tier is the most popular. The $10,000 tier is a good starting point if you are new to prop trading on prediction markets.

All percentage-based rules are the same across every tier. The dollar figures above are derived from the starting balance for each account size.
</Info>

## What happens when you pass

Pass the challenge and PolyFundr automatically opens a new funded account at the same size. There is no second evaluation phase, no manual review, and no delay.

- The funded account carries the same risk rules as the challenge.
- You keep **90% of all realized profits** — open positions do not count until they are closed.
- Request a payout once your realized profit reaches the profit target for the payout cycle.
- Payouts are sent in USDC to your wallet, or by bank transfer.

<Tip>
Drawdown calculations are adjusted for any payouts already made, so withdrawing profits never moves you closer to a drawdown breach on your funded account.
</Tip>

## Free demo account

Every user gets one free **$1,000 demo account** to try the platform before purchasing a challenge. The demo runs for 72 hours and uses the same daily drawdown, total drawdown, and max-risk-per-trade rules as a paid challenge. Demo profits are simulated and not withdrawable.

<Note>
The minimum trading days and inactivity rules do not apply to the demo account. See [Demo Account](/getting-started/demo-account) for the full details.
</Note>
