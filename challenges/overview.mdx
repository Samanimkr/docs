<Info>
All percentage-based rules are the same across every tier. The dollar figures above are derived from the starting balance for each account size.
</Info>

## What is the same on every tier

- Live Polymarket prices
- One-step evaluation
- Same percentage-based rules
- No KYC required to start
- No challenge expiry date
- Immediate activation after payment confirmation

## What happens when you pass

Pass the challenge and PolyFundr automatically opens a funded account at the same size.

- There is no second phase.
- The funded account carries the same drawdown, max-risk, and inactivity rules.
- You keep 90% of funded payouts.
- Payouts are sent in USDC on Polygon.
- Funded payouts use progressive caps: \$1,000 on the 1st request, \$2,000 on
  the 2nd, and \$5,000 from the 3rd request onward.
