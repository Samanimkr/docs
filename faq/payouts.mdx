These are the short answers. For the full policy and examples, use [Funded payouts](/funded/payouts).

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What is the profit split?">
The profit split is **90% to you, 10% to PolyFundr**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When can I request a payout?">
You can request a payout once your realized profit on the funded account reaches the **30% threshold** for that account after prior paid payouts are accounted for. For example, on a \$50,000 funded account, you become eligible once you have \$15,000 in realized profit.

Only realized profit counts. Open positions are excluded until they are closed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I request a payout?">
Submit the request from the **Payouts** page in your dashboard. Enter a destination wallet address, confirm the amount, and submit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a maximum payout request size?">
Yes. The current maximum is **\$1,000 gross per payout request**. At the standard 90% split, a \$1,000 gross request pays \$900 to you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How fast are payouts processed?">
Payouts are processed within **24 hours on average**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What currency are payouts in?">
Payouts are sent in **USDC on the Polygon network**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What kind of wallet address do I need?">
You must provide a valid **EVM wallet address** that can receive **USDC on Polygon**. The destination is required on every request and is not saved as a default.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do open positions count toward my payout?">
No. Only **realized profit from closed positions** counts toward payout eligibility.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to the amount I request while it is being processed?">
The requested amount is **reserved against your available payout balance** while the payout is pending. You cannot request the same gross amount twice while a request is still in progress.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does withdrawing profits affect my drawdown limits?">
No. Paid payouts are accounted for in the drawdown calculations.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any fees on payouts?">
No. There are no payout withdrawal fees.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
