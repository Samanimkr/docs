Once you pass your challenge and start trading on a funded account, payouts are how you withdraw your share of realized profits. The questions below explain how the payout process works, when you can request one, and how funds are sent to you.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What is the profit split?">
The profit split is **90% to you, 10% to PolyFundr**. This applies to all funded accounts regardless of account size. There are no tiered splits or hidden deductions — you keep 90 cents of every dollar of realized profit you earn.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When can I request a payout?">
You can request a payout once your realized profit on the funded account reaches the **30% profit target** for that account. For example, on a $50,000 funded account, you become eligible once you have closed $15,000 in realized profit.

Only realized profit counts — open positions are excluded until they are closed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I request a payout?">
Submit your payout request through the **Payouts** section of your dashboard. Every request requires you to provide a destination wallet address. PolyFundr does not store your destination preference between requests, so you will enter it each time.

Once submitted, your request is reviewed and the funds are sent to the address you provided.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How fast are payouts processed?">
Payouts are processed within **24 hours on average**. There is no fixed schedule — payouts are handled on demand as requests come in.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What currency are payouts in?">
Payouts are sent in **USDC on the Polygon network**. If you prefer, you can also receive your payout by **bank transfer** instead of a crypto wallet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get paid to a bank account?">
Yes. If you prefer not to receive USDC to a wallet, you can request a bank transfer instead. Select your preferred payment method when submitting your payout request through the dashboard.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do open positions count toward my payout?">
No. Only **realized profit from closed positions** counts toward your payout eligibility. Open trades are excluded from the calculation until you close them. This means the profit you see on unrealized positions cannot be withdrawn until those positions are closed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to the amount I request while it is being processed?">
The requested amount is **reserved against your available balance** while the payout is pending. You cannot request the same funds twice while a payout request is in progress.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does withdrawing profits affect my drawdown limits?">
No. Drawdown calculations are adjusted for any payouts already made. Withdrawing your profits will never move you closer to a daily or total drawdown breach — the drawdown floors are recalculated to account for funds you have already taken out.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any fees on payouts?">
No. There are no fees on payout withdrawals. The challenge fee is a one-time cost paid when you purchase your challenge — after that, you simply keep your 90% share of realized profit with no additional deductions.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
