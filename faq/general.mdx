PolyFundr is a prop trading platform for prediction markets. You pass a rules-based challenge to prove your edge, then trade with funded capital of up to $200,000 while keeping 90% of your profits. The questions below cover the basics of how the platform works.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What is PolyFundr?">
PolyFundr is the world's first prop firm for prediction market traders. Most traders are limited by the size of their own bankroll — PolyFundr solves that by backing proven traders with real capital on live Polymarket markets.

You purchase a one-step evaluation challenge, prove you can trade profitably without breaking the rules, and then receive a funded account at the same account size. You keep 90% of any profit you make on the funded account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is Polymarket?">
Polymarket is a leading prediction market platform where traders buy and sell YES/NO outcome shares on real-world events. Prices reflect the market's probability estimate for each outcome, and positions are resolved to $1 (YES) or $0 (NO) when the event settles.

PolyFundr uses live Polymarket prices for all trades. You are not trading on Polymarket directly — PolyFundr simulates your trades against those live prices and tracks your performance within the challenge and funded account rules.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What markets can I trade?">
You can trade any market available on PolyFundr, which mirrors the full range of live Polymarket markets. That includes:

- **Politics** — elections, policy decisions, geopolitical events
- **Crypto** — price milestones, protocol upgrades, regulatory outcomes
- **Sports** — NFL, NBA, Premier League, Formula 1, and more
- **Technology & AI** — product launches, model releases, tech milestones
- **Finance** — economic indicators, central bank decisions
- **Culture** — awards, entertainment, and current events

There are no restrictions on news events, weekends, or holding periods. You can trade any open market at any time and hold positions for as long as you like, as long as you stay within the risk rules.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to complete KYC to start?">
No. There is no KYC (Know Your Customer) verification required to purchase a challenge or begin trading. You can start immediately after signing up.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial or demo account?">
Yes. Every new user gets one free **$1,000 demo account** to try the platform end-to-end. The demo runs for 3 days from the moment you activate it.

The demo applies the same daily drawdown, total drawdown, and max risk-per-trade rules as a paid challenge. Minimum trading days and max inactivity do not apply to the demo. Demo profits are simulated and are not withdrawable.

Each account is limited to one demo. If you want to continue after the demo expires, you can purchase a paid challenge.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I make a profit on PolyFundr?">
You trade YES or NO outcome shares on prediction market events. You profit by closing positions at a better price than you entered:

- Buy YES at a low probability and sell or hold to resolution if the outcome occurs.
- Buy NO at a low probability and sell or hold to resolution if the outcome does not occur.

Only **realized profit** — from closed positions — counts toward your profit target and payout eligibility. Open positions do not count until they are closed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which countries are supported?">
PolyFundr is available to traders in 90+ countries. Because no KYC is required, access is broadly available regardless of where you are located.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I get started?">
Choose a challenge size from $10,000 up to $200,000, pay the one-time challenge fee, and start trading immediately. Once you hit the 30% profit target across at least 7 trading days without breaching any rules, you pass the challenge and receive a funded account automatically — no second phase, no waiting period.
</Accordion>
