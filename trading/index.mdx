Trading on PolyFundr means placing simulated YES or NO trades on real Polymarket prediction markets. No on-chain execution happens when you trade — instead, your position is recorded at the current Polymarket price and your live PnL updates as the market moves. Every trade you place counts against your challenge account, so the same risk rules that govern your challenge also apply to every fill.

The guides in this section cover the full trading experience: how to find markets, how to place a trade step by step, and how to read your positions and PnL once you're in a trade.
