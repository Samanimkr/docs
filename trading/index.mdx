Trading on PolyFundr means placing simulated YES or NO trades on real Polymarket prediction markets. No on-chain execution happens when you trade. Instead, your position is recorded at the current Polymarket price and your live PnL updates as the market moves. Use [Markets](/trading/markets) to learn the event page, [Placing trades](/trading/placing-trades) for the trade flow, and [Positions & PnL](/trading/positions-and-pnl) to understand tracking and portfolio data.
